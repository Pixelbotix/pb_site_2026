Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Methods

PixelBotix ships products through reputed courier and logistics partners. EMS Speed Post may be used on special request for remote or non-serviceable locations. While we aim to ship all items in a single consignment, certain products may be shipped separately due to size, availability, or handling requirements.

2. Shipping Time & Delivery Timeline

Orders are typically shipped on the same business day. Approximately 99% of orders are dispatched within 24 hours on working days (Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays).

Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

Same-day delivery may be available for select locations for orders placed before 1:30 PM. Expected delivery window is between 3:00 PM and 10:00 PM.

While we target delivery within 2-5 business days across India, actual delivery timelines depend on courier service providers and regional logistics constraints.

3. Shipping Charges

Free shipping is available for orders of INR 1000 and above or for products explicitly marked as free shipping.

For orders below INR 1000, the following shipping charges apply:

  • Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Telangana: INR 200
  • All other Indian states and regions: INR 400

4. Express / Next-Day Delivery

Express delivery is available for select NCR locations for prepaid orders at an additional charge of INR 200.

Orders placed before 4:00 PM will be delivered on the next business day. Orders placed after 4:00 PM, or orders requiring customization, may require an additional day.

Express delivery charges are non-refundable in case of cancellation, return, or exchange.

5. Tracking & Notifications

Once an order is dispatched, customers will receive shipment details via email and SMS, including the courier name and tracking (AWB) number.
